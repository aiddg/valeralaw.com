Meet Our Founder, Rev. Arnedo S. Valera

Senior Attorney & Managing Partner

With a distinguished career spanning over three decades, Rev. Arnedo S. Valera brings a rare combination of profound legal acumen and heartfelt compassion to every client's cause. His academic achievements include a Master's Degree in International Affairs, specializing in International Law and Human Rights, from Columbia University in New York.

Admitted to practice before the U.S. Supreme Court, the New York Bar, and the Philippine Bar, as well as numerous U.S. Circuit Courts of Appeals, Rev. Valera's breadth of experience is matched only by his dedication. He also serves as the Executive Director of the Migrant Heritage Commission, championing immigrant rights.
