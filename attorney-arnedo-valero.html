About Rev. Arnedo S. Valera

Rev. Arnedo Silvano Valera is the Senior Attorney and Managing Partner of Valera & Associates PC. With a distinguished career spanning over three decades, he brings a rare combination of profound legal acumen, strategic insight, and heartfelt compassion to every client's cause. Rev. Valera is deeply committed to upholding the rights of immigrants and those facing discrimination, providing expert guidance through the complexities of the U.S. legal system.

His dedication extends beyond the courtroom; he is also the Executive Director of the Migrant Heritage Commission, Inc., further underscoring his commitment to community service and advocacy for immigrant rights.

A Foundation of Excellence & Dedication

Rev. Valera's approach to law is characterized by meticulous preparation, a comprehensive understanding of legal nuances, and an unwavering focus on achieving the best possible outcomes for his clients. He believes in empowering clients with knowledge and providing clear, honest counsel throughout their legal journey. His work is not just a profession but a calling to serve those in need of strong legal advocacy.

Key Practice Areas

  • Complex Removal and Deportation Defense
  • Asylum, Withholding of Removal, and Convention Against Torture Claims
  • Federal Court Litigation and Appeals (including U.S. Supreme Court)
  • Family-Based and Employment-Based Immigration Petitions
  • Non-Immigrant Visas (Work, Student, Visitor, Fiancé(e))
  • Victim-Based Visas (U Visa, T Visa)
  • Naturalization and Citizenship
  • Anti-Discrimination and Human Rights Law
  • International Trade and Contracts
