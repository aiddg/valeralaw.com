About Rev. Arnedo S. Valera

Rev. Arnedo Silvano Valera is the Senior Attorney and Managing Partner of Valera & Associates PC, a U.S. immigration and anti-discrimination law firm he has led for **over 32 years**. With a distinguished career marked by a profound commitment to justice, he brings a rare combination of deep legal acumen, strategic insight, and heartfelt compassion to every client's cause. Rev. Valera is dedicated to upholding the rights of immigrants and those facing discrimination, providing expert guidance through the complexities of the U.S. legal system.

His dedication extends beyond the courtroom; he also serves as the **Executive Director of the Global Migrant Heritage Foundation**, further underscoring his commitment to community service and advocacy for immigrant rights.

A Foundation of Excellence & Dedication

Rev. Valera's approach to law is characterized by meticulous preparation, a comprehensive understanding of legal nuances, and an unwavering focus on achieving the best possible outcomes for his clients. He believes in empowering clients with knowledge and providing clear, honest counsel throughout their legal journey. His academic pursuits began with a Bachelor of Arts in Philosophy from the University of Santo Tomas and a Bachelor of Laws from Ateneo de Manila University in the Philippines. This was further enriched by specialized training in International Law at the International Institute of Human Rights in Strasbourg, France, and culminated in a **Master's Degree in International Affairs and International Law and Human Rights from Columbia University** in New York.

His work is not just a profession but a calling to serve those in need of strong legal advocacy, particularly within the Filipino community and beyond.
