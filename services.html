Areas of Dedication & Expertise

We are committed to providing specialized legal support tailored to your individual needs and circumstances across these critical areas:

I. Removal Proceedings

Navigating the complexities of deportation defense with strategic representation in immigration court, covering withholding of removal, asylum, waivers, and more.

Explore Removal Defense

II. BIA Appeals

Expert appellate advocacy before the Board of Immigration Appeals, challenging unfavorable decisions and seeking justice.

Learn About BIA Practice

III. Federal & Supreme Court

High-level representation in U.S. Federal Courts and the Supreme Court for impactful immigration-related cases.

Federal Court Representation

IV. Non-Immigrant Visas

Comprehensive assistance for various temporary visas, including work (H, L, E, O, P), student (F-1), visitor (B1/B2), victim (U/T), and more.

Discover Visa Options

V. Green Cards & Citizenship

Guiding clients through the intricate process of obtaining lawful permanent residency (Green Card) and U.S. citizenship (Naturalization).

Your Path to Citizenship

VI. Employment & Family Petitions

Expertise in PERM labor certification, employment-based immigrant petitions, and family-sponsored immigration processes for spouses, children, and parents.

Employment & Family Visas

Navigating the legal requirements for dual citizenship, with a focus on Philippines & United States nationality laws.

Providing legal counsel for international business transactions, contract drafting, and negotiation.
