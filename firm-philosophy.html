Our Mission: Justice, Dignity, and Results

At Valera & Associates PC, our mission is to provide exceptional legal representation in immigration and anti-discrimination law, grounded in a deep commitment to justice, human dignity, and achieving tangible results for our clients. We understand that navigating the legal system can be daunting, especially when facing matters that profoundly impact one's life, family, and future. Our philosophy is built upon providing not only expert legal counsel but also compassionate support and clear guidance every step of the way.

For over three decades, we have dedicated ourselves to being more than just lawyers; we strive to be trusted advocates and partners for those we serve. We believe that everyone deserves access to skilled legal representation and a fair chance to pursue their aspirations and protect their rights.

Our Core Values

Unyielding Advocacy

We are tenacious in our pursuit of justice for our clients, employing strategic thinking and vigorous representation in every case.

Client-Centered Service

Our clients are at the heart of everything we do. We listen, we understand, and we tailor our approach to meet their unique needs and goals.

Integrity & Ethics

We uphold the highest standards of professionalism, honesty, and ethical conduct in all our interactions and legal practice.

Expertise & Innovation

We continuously deepen our knowledge of evolving laws and leverage innovative strategies to address complex legal challenges effectively.

Community & Compassion

We are committed to serving our community and approach each case with empathy, recognizing the human stories behind the legal issues.

A Message from Our Founder

"We call for peace and solidarity in these trying times. We are not to lose faith in our capacity to build a kind and compassionate world, in serving humanity the best way we can... The world is a web of interdependent humanity that our assurance of full security and happiness is not decided by us alone but in the end, rests with relationship that we build and which are founded precisely on justice, freedom and human trust." - Rev. Arnedo S. Valera (Excerpt from "A Prayer Message of Peace")

This sentiment, penned by our founder, Rev. Arnedo S. Valera, encapsulates the spirit that drives our firm. It reflects our belief in the interconnectedness of humanity and the fundamental importance of justice, freedom, and trust in all that we do. We are dedicated to being a source of strength and expert guidance for those navigating the often challenging paths of immigration and anti-discrimination law.
